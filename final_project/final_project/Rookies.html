Rookies:

This section contains rookies of star players and players with the potential to become stars. Rookie cards are highly sought after because they are the first card produced of the certain player.

Legend

  • Type: What card set is it from
  • Graded: On a Scale to 1-10, how perfect is the condition
  • Estimated Price: If sold, how much would it approximately sell for
  • Year: The year the card was created
  • #: What the serial number is, ex: 116/299
  • Parallel: What the color/design of the card is
