Old School Cards:

This section contains cards that where produced before 2015, and way before the sports cards boom. These cards are very rare, and are of well known stars or superstars.

Legend

  • Type: What card set is it from
  • Graded: On a Scale to 1-10, how perfect is the condition
  • Estimated Price: If sold, how much would it approximately sell for
  • Year: The year the card was created
  • #: What the serial number is, ex: 117/299
  • Parallel: What the color/design of the card is
