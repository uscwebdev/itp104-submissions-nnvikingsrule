High End Cards:

This section contains the most prized cards in this collection, ranging from Hall of Famers to Current Superstars to Future Hall of Famers! All of these cards hold a value of more than $100, making them collectors items.

Legend

  • Type: What card set is it from
  • Graded: On a Scale to 1-10, how perfect is the condition
  • Estimated Price: If sold, how much would it approximately sell for
  • Year: The year the card was created
  • #: What the serial number is, ex: 115/299
  • Parallel: What the color/design of the card is
