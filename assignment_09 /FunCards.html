Fun Cards:

This section contains cards of players that didn't quite fulfill there full potential and cards with very unique designs. While these cards may not be worth a lot of money, to collectors, they are worth much more than the monteary value!

Legend

  • Type: What card set is it from
  • Graded: On a Scale to 1-10, how perfect is the condition
  • Estimated Price: If sold, how much would it approximately sell for
  • Year: The year the card was created
  • #: What the serial number is, ex: 118/299
  • Parallel: What the color/design of the card is
